Pancakes Recipe

I actually don't know how to make pancakes.

Just buy the pancakes from the frozen food aisle.

Second paragraph, I like pancakes.

Ingredients

  • Pancakes, frozen food, already cooked
  • Butter
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Put pancakes on stove, cook for a few minutes.
  2. Sprinkle on Cinnamon both sides.
  3. Throw in butter for smooth taste.
  4. Cook both sides.
  5. Eat it. So easy.
