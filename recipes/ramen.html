Ramen Recipe

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Noodles
  • Soup Packet
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Boil water.
  2. Put in soup base.
  3. Cook noodles.
  4. Throw in egg.
  5. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Eat it. So easy.
